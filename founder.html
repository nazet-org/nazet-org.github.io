Meet Our Founder Shelomith Watson

Certified Equestrian Specialist (ES)

Shelo is a Certified EAGALA Equine Specialist. For Shelo horses has been her main passion in life since receiving her first pony "Goldie" at 6-years of age. She resides with her husband and 4 sons as a resident of the Flagstaff area for the past 20 years where she has raised several different breeds of horses and also become a certified trainer in equestrian dressage.

Together with her family, friends and a team of equestrian professionals, Shelo maintains the friendly spirit in the local equestrian community and is an active member in the Church Of Jesus Christ Of Latter-Day Saints. She has achieved a lot in her years equestrian pursuits but she is planning to achieve even more during the years to come helping those in her community through Equine Therapy.

Our mission

A safe and nurturing therapeutic environment providing all those we work with – including our clients, their caregivers, our human and animal partners and colleagues – with the context, rest, support and intervention required for insight, healing and growth to arise.

Values

Our aim at Nazet.org is to safeguard, and where possible improve, the safety and well being (physical and emotional) of our clients, colleagues and animal partners. Our Code of Ethics reflects our values and help us achieve this goal. Anyone working with Nazet.org clients and/or horses, or other animals whether as partner, employee, volunteer or intern is required to read and adhere to the following Code of Ethics.

Code Of Ethics

- We treat other counselors, mental health and learning professionals, especially those in the same area of practice as ourselves, as colleagues rather than as competitors.

- We share resources and ideas with other counselors, mental health and learning professionals.

- We treat all other counselors, mental health and learning professionals, and their ideas and suggestions with respect and courtesy.

- We respect differences in counselling and education approach, style and philosophy and do not try to impose our rolex approach or beliefs as better or more ‘right’ than anyone else’s.

- We provide other individuals seeking to work in this area with support, guidance and resources.

- We ensure our training workshops are fairly priced and do not take financial advantage of people’s desire to learn.

- We treat our volunteers with respect and appreciation.

Pursuit Of Excellence

We try to improve the equine industry through working with other organizations to achieve shared goals and ideals.
