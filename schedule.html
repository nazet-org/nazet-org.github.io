Schedule

2017 Course Schedule

On this page, you can read more about our schedule for this year’s group courses and trainings.

Throughout the year we will offer a range of courses, from Beginning Level 1 through Level 4+ topics, and from 1-week to 10-week courses. These courses may include the opportunity to ride with, or audit, Pat and Linda Parelli. Topics include Riding with Confidence, Liberty & Horse Behavior, Colt Starting, and Level/Savvy-specific 1-week courses.

For those students who have not yet started their Professional Pathway, and are interested in becoming a Horserati Instructor, ask our administrators about the Professionals Pathway. If you have already attended a Fast Track (or beyond) and have specific questions about your next steps, please contact us.
